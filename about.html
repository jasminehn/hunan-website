About Us

 

Hunan Coventry is a modest, local Chinese restaurant with a clean and welcoming atmosphere. We are family owned, family run business that has been operating in Cleveland for over 30 years. We serve traditional Hunan cuisine, as well as other Asian-inspired dishes. Our food is not only deicious, but also at a reasonable price. The authentic tastes of our food will make you feel like you are in China!
